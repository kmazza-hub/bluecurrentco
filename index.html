FAQ

Quick answers contractors usually want up front.

Do I need a new website?

Usually not. Most contractors just need better clarity, trust signals, and a cleaner “contact me” path. We improve what you already have unless a rebuild is truly the fastest option.

How fast can you turn things around?

Many fixes can be done in days. Bigger refreshes depend on scope, but you’ll always get a clear plan and timeline.

Do I own the site and content?

Yes. You keep full ownership. No “held hostage” setups. Clean handoff every time.

Do you do ongoing monthly retainers?

Not required. Most work is done as a clear project. If you want ongoing help later, we can do that—but only if it makes sense.

What do you need from me?

Your site link, service area, and the type of jobs you want more of. If we move forward, photos and basic details help—nothing crazy.

Is this “SEO”?

This is about clarity and trust first—then basic alignment with Google (consistent services, service area, and messaging). Real SEO can come later if needed.
